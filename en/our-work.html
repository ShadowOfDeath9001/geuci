UFU GRADUATION INTERNATIONAL MOBILITY FLOW

The “Report on International Mobilities for Graduation of the Federal University of Uberlândia – Sending and Receiving” was produced in 2018 and involved the gathering of gross data on international mobilities related to UFU from 2007 to 2017, leading to the elaboration of an interactive world map displaying the analysed data. It shows the chosen destination of our outgoing exchange students and also the country of origin of our incoming students. The report itself consists of a detailed analysis of the aforementioned map and gathered data. Together with a historical-junctural research, the report seeks to understand the student flow of the university and how the juncture at the federal level affects it (considering especially the impacts of federal student exchange programmes).


UFU INTERNATIONAL AGREEMENTS

The “Report on Ongoing and Expired International Agreements of the Federal University of Uberlândia” was produced in 2018, with its elaboration consisting of three phases. The first dealt with the gathering of gross data on the international agreements held by the university, enabling us to separate those that were still active from those expired. The second phase consisted of elaborating an interactive world map for the plotting of the data gathered, showing the countries with which the university has agreements with. The final phase consisted of the actual report writing, which consists of a detailed analysis of the map and gathered data, identifying in a comparative way the agreements held by the university over the years by means of a continental approach.
