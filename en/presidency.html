Presidency

To guarantee the feasibility of GEUCI projects, a dedicated and responsible team is a must. That team consists of the Presidency and Vice-Presidency posts, with their joint work comprising an important part of the organisation’s effectiveness and reach.

Both members below have contributed towards the production of reports and projects in collaboration with the Economic Development Council of Uberlândia (CODEN), with the International Relations Directory of the Federal University of Uberlândia (UFU), and with SEBRAE, striving to promote the internationalisation of Uberlândia.


Diandra Schatz
  PRESIDENT  
Currently the group president and International Relations undergraduate, she has been a member of GEUCI since 2016.


Laura Hemilly
  VICE-PRESIDENT
